Almond Berry Smoothie Recipe

Almond milk and almond butter are the star ingredients in this berry
smoothie with almond milk for a nutritious, on-the-go meal that is vegan
and paleo-friendly.


Preparation time | Total time | Servings
10 min. 10 min. 1

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • water as needed

Directions

Steps

  1. Combine blueberries, banana, almond milk, and almond butter in a
    blender; blend until smooth, adding water for a thinner smoothie.
