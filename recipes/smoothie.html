Fruit and Yogurt Smoothie Recipe

This yogurt smothie recipe is delicious! You may substitute the
strawberries for any other berries or fruit.


Preparation time | Total time | Servings
5 min. 5 min. 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

Directions

Step 1

Combine strawberries, banana, yogurt, pineapple juice, sugar,
orangejuice and milk in a blender. Blend until smooth.
